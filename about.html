About the StudyRoom

This StudyRoom is created as a project for the Tic Bootcamp 2022 to help manage the problem of study groups in schools and teachers that are not accessible to teach. With this platform, problems as such will be minimised and will help reduce the non-termination of the syllabi in schools and training institutions like the Tic Bootcamp itself.
